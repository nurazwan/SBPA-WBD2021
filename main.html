The Singapore Book Publishers Association (SBPA) is celebrating UNESCO World Book and Copyright Day 2021 on Friday 23 April with a book bazaar promotion and copyright school challenge. The Book Bazaar will be held from Friday 23 April 2021 to Tuesday 2 May 2021.

15 publishers will be taking part in this virtual event on bookbazaar.sg and powered by goguru.com

A series of webinars organised by SBPA for publishers and the public. Enjoy discounts of up to 40% for books purchased from participating publishers during this period. An exclusive promotion from Storytel Singapore for 30 days of unlimited audiobooks.

Join the livestreams hosted by participating publishers over weekends of the book bazaar. These will cover book launches and an educational talk. Visitors and audiences can interact directly with the publishers over Facebook LIVE and pose questions or comments that they may have about the content on stream.
